My time volunteering for this association

Since 2013, I committed a significant part of my life to volunteering for the association Mission MVC that supports poor families in South America by providing pre-fabricated houses to them. During these several years as a volunteer and fundraiser I have learnt the beauty of making a purpose-driven impact.

Since the first time I went to Peru in 2013, I’ve been an active member of the fundraising team and in 2019 we accomplished to raise €185k for the 2019 volunteering projects in Peru and Colombia consiting on the construction of 20+ pre-fabricated houses.

“Sosteniamoci”, a project launched during Covid-19 pandemic

During the quarantine we launched a project called “Sosteniamoci” that was founded with the fundamental aim of providing basic necessities to families economically affected by COVID-19. Since June we have organized 5 rounds of deliveries to 150+ families in Milan and Rome but we are now finishing our funds.

Please visit the website (it’s in italian but it’s intuitive ;) and if you like our project, make a donation here: https://www.sosteniamoci.org/store/
